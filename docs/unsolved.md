### 3A.17

Suppose $𝑉$ is finite-dimensional. Show that the only two-sided 
ideals of $ℒ(𝑉)$ are $\{0\}$ and $ℒ(𝑉)$.

A subspace $ℰ$ of $ℒ(𝑉)$ is called a two-sided ideal of $ℒ(𝑉)$ if
$𝑇𝐸 ∈ ℰ$ and $𝐸𝑇 ∈ ℰ$ for all $𝐸 ∈ ℰ$ and all $𝑇 ∈ ℒ(𝑉)$.

### 3B.25
